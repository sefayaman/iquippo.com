Valuation

  • In today’s competitive and unpredictable business world, it is essential for companies to have accurate, timely and cost-effective valuations of their machinery and equipment.
  • At iQuippo, our experienced team of equipment valuation experts is dedicated to providing our clients with an objective & supportable valuation conclusions, which are provided in timely, well-organized appraisal reports.
  • There are a wide variety of reasons in which an organization may require equipment valuation services, including the desire or need to sell, wanting to purchase additional equipment etc. This service can be availed from both Buyer’s and Seller’s side.

Our team of experts have developed a comprehensive checklist of all major and minor components and conduct a detailed check-up of each of these, including:

  • Cabin : Dashboard, Meters & Gauges, Steering Wheel, Cabin Canopy, etc.
  • Engine : Main Engine, Fuel Pump, Alternator, Radiator, Battery, Self-Starter, etc.
  • Undercarriage : Track Motors, Track Pads, Track Rollers, Sprocket, etc.
  • Bucket : Basic Bucket, Bucket Teeth, Grease Points, Pins & Bushes.
  • Hydraulic System : Oil Tank, Hydraulic Pump, Control Valve, Boom Cylinder, Pipe Lines, etc.
  • Trolley : Trolley Walls, Axles, Floors, Tires.
  • Chassis : Chassis Frame, Leaf Springs, Shock Absorbers, etc.

Depending on the nature of machinery, a host of other components are also checked and evaluated.
